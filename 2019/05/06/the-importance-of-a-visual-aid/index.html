The Importance of a Visual Aid

2019-05-06

Visualations are the easiest way to get a point across or to tell a story. One’s visual can make or break a presentation, so its safe to say that you shouldn’t take them lightly. Its important to take in to acount what your visiaul is telling the world and whether its telling the world the correct thing.

plot3<-ggplot(facebook, aes(party))+ geom_bar()+ ggtitle("Politcal Party")


plot2<-ggplot(facebook, aes(party, fill= party))+ geom_bar()+ ggtitle("Politcal Party")+ 
scale_fill_manual("legend", values = c("Democratic Party" = "blue", "Republican Party" = "red")) + theme(legend.position = "none")


grid.arrange(plot2,plot3, ncol=2)

If you direct your eyes to the graph above you’ll see a demonstration of how something so simple such as adding color can enhance the quality of a graph. Not only does it draw the eye but i chose colors that go with the levels because democrats are often associted with the color blue and republican red.
