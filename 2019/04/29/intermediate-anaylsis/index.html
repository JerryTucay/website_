K-Fold Cross-Validation

The k-fold method involves randomly dividing the set of observations into k groups or folds of approximately equal sizes. The process involves all but one using those to train your data and then using that one to test the efficiency of your model. After averaging the mean standard error from each of the k tests we can tell how well our model is predicting. Lower the number indicating a better model.

set.seed(74747)
glm_fit <- glm(local_topic~oppose_any, data= facebook)
cv.error <- cv.glm(facebook, glm_fit, K= 6 )$delta[1]
cv.error
## [1] 0.01333858
